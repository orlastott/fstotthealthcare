Optometry Services for Prisons

F Stott Healthcare Ltd delivers high‑quality, community‑equivalent optometry services within prison settings, ensuring people in custody receive timely, professional eye care that meets NHS standards.

Our Services

  • Full NHS General Ophthalmic Services (GOS) sight tests
  • Diagnosis and management of common eye conditions
  • Hospital referrals for urgent or specialist care
  • Prescription and dispensing of spectacles
  • Glaucoma, cataract and retinal disease monitoring
  • Enhanced services such as MECS‑equivalent assessments and post‑operative reviews

How We Work

We run regular on‑site clinics within prison healthcare units using portable diagnostic equipment. Appointments are arranged through healthcare teams or prisoner self‑referral. Our clinicians are GOC‑registered, security‑cleared and trained in safeguarding and information governance, ensuring safe and compliant care.

Benefits for Prisons

  • Reduces the need for hospital escorts and security staffing
  • Improves access to essential healthcare services
  • Supports rehabilitation, education and daily living
  • Facilitates earlier detection of sight‑threatening conditions

Quality & Governance

F Stott Healthcare Ltd works to a robust clinical governance framework, ensuring:

  • Compliance with GOC and NHS England standards
  • Secure, confidential handling of patient information
  • Regular audits, performance monitoring and patient feedback

Why Choose F Stott Healthcare Ltd

With over 25 years of experience in primary eye care and leadership across Cheshire and Merseyside, we offer dependable, expert, and compassionate care designed to reduce health inequalities for people in custody.

Domiciliary Care

F Stott Healthcare Ltd provides comprehensive domiciliary eye examinations for individuals unable to visit an optometry practice due to health or mobility challenges. Our experienced optometrist, Fionnuala Stott, delivers personalised, high‑quality eye care in the comfort of your own home.

  • Home‑based eye examinations with no compromise on clinical quality
  • A warm, patient‑centred approach tailored to your needs
  • Consistent care, with every visit carried out by Fionnuala Stott
  • NHS domiciliary eye tests for those who qualify
  • Private home eye examinations also available

NHS Domiciliary Eye Examinations

An NHS home eye test is available to individuals who are unable to attend an optometry practice due to health or mobility issues. Fionnuala Stott is fully qualified and approved to provide these examinations in the comfort of your own home.

You may be entitled if you qualify for a free NHS sight test and cannot attend a practice unaccompanied due to health or mobility reasons. If you’re unsure, we’re happy to advise.

Private Domiciliary Eye Examinations

If you do not meet the NHS criteria—or prefer greater flexibility—we also offer private domiciliary eye examinations. These provide the same comprehensive, personalised clinical care from Fionnuala Stott, arranged at your convenience.
