About Fionnuala

Fionnuala Stott is an experienced optometrist, healthcare leader, and advocate for high‑quality, accessible eye care. With over 25 years of clinical experience across Cheshire, she has built a strong reputation for professionalism, collaboration, and a commitment to improving patient outcomes.


Throughout her career, Fionnuala has held several key leadership roles, including serving as the Optometry representative on the System Primary Care Committee for Cheshire and Merseyside ICB, chairing the Cheshire Local Optical Committee, and being a founder member of the board at Primary Eyecare Services. Her work has contributed significantly to the development of local eye‑care pathways and strengthened partnerships across primary and secondary care.


Having lived and worked in Cheshire for more than two decades, Fionnuala brings deep local knowledge and a strong connection to the communities she serves. She founded F Stott Healthcare Ltd to provide patient‑centred optometry services with a focus on equity, accessibility, and clinical excellence.


Today, Fionnuala continues to champion improvements in eye health across Cheshire and Merseyside, delivering services that ensure safe, compassionate care — including dedicated optometry provision within prison settings for underserved populations.
